About

Our company provides quality programming and development services for the video game industry. Our team of experienced and talented programmers has a proven track record of success, having worked on critically acclaimed titles such as Baldur's Gate 3 and Wasteland 3. We specialize in crafting high-quality, engaging and immersive games and simulations that are tailored to delight players across multiple platforms including PC, mobile, VR and more. We are proficient in using popular game engines like Unreal Engine and Unity, as well as custom-built engines. Our ultimate goal is to empower game developers and simulation creators with the tools and expertise they need to bring their ideas to life and create experiences that will stand the test of time and be accessible to everyone.

Gameplay Programming

We specialize in gameplay programming and work together with our clients to implement, and test gameplay mechanics and features. We can create engaging and dynamic gameplay mechanics that will captivate players. From character movement to quests and inventory systems, we can help you design and develop the foundation of your game or simulation.

Graphics Programming

We offer graphics programming services for the video game industry. Our team of expert programmers are well-versed in the use of shaders, lighting, and materials to create advanced visual effects and ensure optimal performance on various platforms.

Backend and Networking

We design and implement the server infrastructure necessary for multiplayer games and other online features. We use a variety of programming languages and tools to create stable, scalable, and secure backend systems. Our goal is to help game developers create seamless online experiences for their players.

Engine Programming

Our company has experience designing, implementing, and maintaining the underlying game engine that powers a game. This can include creating custom game engines from scratch, or modifying and extending existing engines to create the technical foundation for game developers to bring their ideas to life.

Optimization and bugfixes

Our team will identify and fix bottlenecks, bugs and glitches that can impact player experience. From memory management to code refactoring, we will make sure your game runs smoothly and efficiently, providing the players with a polished and enjoyable experience on any platform.

Porting

We assist game and simulation developers in expanding the reach of their projects to new platforms. We have the expertise to take a game designed for one platform and make it compatible with another, while maintaining the quality and features of the original product. We have experience in porting games and simulations to various platforms, including PC, consoles, mobile, and VR.

Portfolio

Earthlings

Earthlings is a social MMO built using Unreal Engine 5 where you'll have the freedom to create and customize your own land plots, visit a variety of unique and immersive environments, and interact with other players in real time. With our team's expertise in gameplay, graphics and backend programming, we are building a game that will be a social and immersive experience, that allows players to express themselves, create and collaborate in a vast and living world.

We got put in charge of all development by our client and are working on implementing various different tasks such as character movement and abilities, Server transitions and back-end, Mobs and NPCs, Inventory, Questing system, Vehicles and so much more!

VR4Fire

VR4Fire is a VR fire simulation we developed for our client using Unity, designed to teach individuals the appropriate actions to take during fire emergencies in various scenarios. This simulation immerses users in a realistic virtual environment, allowing them to experience a fire breakout and practice their response in a safe and controlled setting. The simulation includes multiple scenarios, such as hospitals and warehouses, and users can experience the spread of fire and smoke, as well as learn how to use fire safety equipment.
